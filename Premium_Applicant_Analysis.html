Key Finding: Lower engagement in Feb 2026 vs Feb 2025

Web applications per applicant fell to 5.10 in Feb 2026 vs 5.54 in Feb 2025 (–8%). Total applications per applicant dropped even sharper: 4.55 vs 5.13 (–11%). After Feb 3, total applications averaged 13,971/day (weekdays) vs 15,298 on Feb 2 — though the Feb 2 sample is a single Monday. Web applicant volumes recovered strongly from Jan 2026 (+19.6%) but engagement depth (applies per visitor) weakened.
